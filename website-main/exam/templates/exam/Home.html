WHY AIM2CRACK?

No explanation to the answers?

Aim2crackprovides you detailed explanation of the concept.

Calling a friend in last two minutes and getting the answers?

Aim2crackquestions are timed and random, answers can't be easily copied.

Searching the question in google?

Aim2crack prevents changing of tabs and test will be submitted once the tab is changed

Searching in a second device

Aim2crackaids the faculty to find the time taken to get the answer in google. Faculty can set the timer accordingly.

Filling the numerical easily with the help of a friend?

Aim2crackquestions involve random number generation which gives different numerical value for different students.

Log in through a friend

Aim2crack detects multiple login and notification will be sent to faculty.
